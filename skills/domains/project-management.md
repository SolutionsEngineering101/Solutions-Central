# Skill Domain: Project Management

## What this covers
Sprint planning, backlog grooming, task tracking, stakeholder communication, risk management, and delivery coordination across internal and client-facing projects.

## Level Guide

| Level | What you can do |
|---|---|
| 1 | Understands sprint structure and can update task status |
| 2 | Manages own tasks and deadlines; participates in sprint ceremonies |
| 3 | Runs sprints for a workstream; proactively flags blockers; writes sprint reports |
| 4 | Manages cross-functional delivery; drives process improvements; mentors PMs |
