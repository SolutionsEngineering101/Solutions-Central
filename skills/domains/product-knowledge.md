# Skill Domain: Product Knowledge

## What this covers
Deep understanding of Vantage Circle platform modules: Rewards & Recognition, Perks, Survey, Corporate Wellness, and how they interact with client configurations, billing, and customizations.

## Level Guide

| Level | What you can do |
|---|---|
| 1 | Knows the module names and high-level purpose |
| 2 | Can walk a client through features; knows standard configurations |
| 3 | Understands customization limits, edge cases, and client-specific setups |
| 4 | Can spec new features; advises on product roadmap impact for clients |

- `product-information/` — feature specs, release notes, screenshots
- `documents/` — BRDs, PRDs for platform features
