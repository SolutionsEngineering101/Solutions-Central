# Skill Domain: Client Onboarding

## What this covers
End-to-end go-live process: requirement gathering, SPOC coordination, configuration setup, UAT, data migration, training, and handover.

## Level Guide

| Level | What you can do |
|---|---|
| 1 | Understands the onboarding checklist and timeline |
| 2 | Can manage a standard onboarding with supervision |
| 3 | Runs complex onboardings independently; handles escalations |
| 4 | Defines onboarding playbooks; trains new team members; handles enterprise clients |

## Key Resources
- `playbook/entries/` — recorded onboarding learnings
- `pre-built-solutions/blueprints/` — reusable onboarding solution templates
- `intake/solutions-forms/` — historical client requests for reference
