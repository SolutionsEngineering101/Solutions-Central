# Solution Request #238 - Servier Les Lab
**Date:** 2026-04-16  |  **Status:** Open  |  **Complexity:** Not Set

## Subject
Requirement Discussion: Progressive Panel Award Workflow

## Brief
Requirement Summary:
Client requires configuration of a Progressive Panel Award. In the standard workflow, a nominator submits a nomination and the nominee’s Head of Department approves/declines, with nominators allowed to nominate only cross-functional team members.

For the Progressive Panel Award, the requirement is that the winner of the previous award cycle is auto-nominated for the panel award. Additionally, panel voters should not be able to view nominations from their own department (i.e., voters can only view nominations from other departments).

Query:
Please confirm feasibility of this setup and suggest any alternate approaches that could achieve similar functionality if direct configuration is not possible.

## Problem Description
I would like to bring up a requirement from a new client regarding a Progressive Panel Award and seek your inputs on the feasibility and any possible alternate approaches.

Currently, the workflow for the standard award is as follows: the nominator submits a nomination, and the nominee’s Head of Department reviews and either approves or declines the nomination. Additionally, one of the nomination criteria is that nominators are allowed to nominate only employees from cross-functional teams.

For the Progressive Panel Award, the client’s requirement is that the winner of the previous award cycle should be auto-nominated for the subsequent panel award. However, there is an added condition that panel voters should not be able to view nominations from their own department. In other words, panel members should only be able to view and vote on nominations belonging to departments other than their own.

Could you please review this requirement and share your thoughts on the feasibility of configuring this workflow? Additionally, it would be helpful if you could suggest any alternate approaches that could achieve a similar outcome, in case the above setup is not directly feasible.

## Solution Given
Our Understanding: The client wants to configure Progressive Panel Awards with the following conditions:

For the First award, only cross-department nominations should be allowed. 
Approval should go to the Nominee's HOD. 
Winners should be directly nominated for the Panel Award.
Panel Voters should not receive nominations from their own department.

We will discuss this with the concerned teams and get back to you.
