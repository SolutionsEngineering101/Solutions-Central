# Solution Request #149 - Kotak & Tata Technologies
**Date:** 2025-10-10  |  **Status:** Solution Given Closed  |  **Complexity:** Medium Complexity

## Subject
Solution Request 154149 Enabling 'Assign Nominator' functionality for Badges

## Brief
enabling selected email for non monetary nominations instead of department/grade group

## Problem Description
specific email id should have access to send NM recognition, right now we don't have this filter option enabled

## Solution Given
We’ve discussed this internally and with the tech team, and there are a few key considerations before proceeding with any customizations for the client.

Our core Rewards & Recognition (R&R) philosophy is centered around enabling non-monetary appreciation for all employees. While we understand that organizations may have department-specific skills or behavioral traits they wish to highlight, which is why we offer advanced filtering for badges, restricting access to badges for only a select group goes against the inclusive nature of our R&R approach.

Additionally, the expected usage volume of such badges - both in terms of the number created and how frequently they’d be used - may be low, requesting you to share more details on this.

Lastly, since these are non-monetary recognitions, investing significant effort into customization may not be optimal from a business value perspective.
