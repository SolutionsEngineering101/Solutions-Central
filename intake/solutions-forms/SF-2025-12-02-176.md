## Problem Description
As informed to Hemanga, Hyundai Capital has 9 employees who are OOO right now.
These employees are unable to access their work email (firstname.lastname@hcs.com) due to which they wont be able to redeem their points (as gift card OTPs are sent to email). Can these users receive OTPs over whatsapp if their numbers were available to us ?

## Solution Given
Our platform can send OTPs via WhatsApp for non-network employees. However, for network employees, OTPs can only be delivered to their registered official email ID, as this is the standard security requirement.
From the information we have, it appears that these 9 employees may fall under the network employee category, since they are registered with official Hyundai Capital email addresses. If this is correct, WhatsApp OTP delivery would not be available for them.
That said, if you could please confirm whether these employees are indeed non-network employees, we would proceed to enable WhatsApp OTP delivery for them.
