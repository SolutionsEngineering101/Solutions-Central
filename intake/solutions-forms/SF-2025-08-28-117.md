# Solution Request #117 - Tech Mahindra
**Date:** 2025-08-28  |  **Status:** Solution Given Closed  |  **Complexity:** Low Complexity

## Subject
Solution request124117::Exploring Secure Access Options for Tech Mahindra’s Vantage Perks Platform

## Brief
One of my prospects, Tech Mahindra, requires a secure way to restrict access to the Vantage Perks platform only for their active employees. Since they are not opting for SSO or HRIS integration at this stage, they want to understand alternative authentication mechanisms that can achieve this. A short call between our Solutions Team and their POC will be of great help in this case.

## Problem Description
While I have suggested options like domain restriction, whitelisting, and bulk CSV upload to control user access, the client is specifically interested in exploring OTP-based authentication and understanding how it can work with Vantage Circle. They are seeking an expert solution from your end to evaluate their authentication needs in detail and recommend the best way forward.

## Solution Given
OTP login is available as a backup option, primarily used in scenarios where users have forgotten their passwords. To enhance this into a complete two-factor authentication (2FA) solution—where OTPs are sent to users' corporate email addresses—would require further development effort across both the web and mobile platforms.
On a related note, while the mobile app currently maintains active sessions until users manually log out, there’s no automatic timeout feature in place. In contrast, the web platform typically enforces a standard 30-minute inactivity timeout. To meet the client’s request for re-authentication prior to any transaction, implementing session expiration on mobile would also involve further development effort.
