**Date:** 2025-11-07  |  **Status:** To Product Closed  |  **Complexity:** High Complexity

## Subject
JIRA : VC -14023 - Mitsubishi Chemicals America

## Brief
Allow winner selector to be nominated for a panel award

## Problem Description
Jira : VC-14023

## Solution Given
We’d like to clarify a few important points regarding the award nomination, panel voting and winner selection process:
Conflict of Interest in Winner Selection: A person who is designated as a winner selector cannot be nominated for the same award. This is to avoid any potential bias and to maintain the integrity of the selection process.
Panelist Nominations: Similarly, if a nominator puts forward a panelist as a nominee, it creates a situation where the panelist could potentially vote for themselves. Since nominations are not shared among panelists to preserve impartiality, this would conflict with the core philosophy of panel-based awards.Request for Clarification: We need more details about the client’s intended workflow, including:
Type and structure of panels
Department-specific setup
Identities of panelists and winner selectors
Existing rules or patterns in the current award process
