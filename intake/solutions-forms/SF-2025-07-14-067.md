# Solution Request #67 - Antara Senior Care
**Date:** 2025-07-14  |  **Status:** Solution Given Closed  |  **Complexity:** Medium Complexity

## Subject
Feasibility check for Panal Award | Antara Senior Care

## Brief
Workflow feasibility check with the specific user information

## Problem Description
The client needs to include a specific group of employees in the Panel Awards. Under the business lines 'AGEasy' and 'NotAGEasy' there are two groups. Frontlines and non frontlines
>>In one award the eleigible recipients will be frontlines who fall under 'NotAGEasy' lines.
>>For another award, the eligible recipients are non front lines who fall under the same 'NotAGEasy' group
>>For one more award, the eligible recioients are the whole members of 'AGEasy' line
Now, we are acquaring the 'AGEasy' and 'NotAGEasy' information in additional info 1 column and acquaring the 'Frontlines' and 'Nonfrontlines' groups in additional info column 2.
The user specific information are taken in single columns.
Kindly confirm how effectively we can achive the requested workflo for the Panel Award.

## Solution Given
The Antara Senior Care Panel Award workflow can be customized based on the Business Unit (BU) and Employee Category. Use the "Receiver Entity Type" in the advanced filter to assign awards by BU (e.g., Not AGEasy or AGEasy). The option "The individuals nominated for this award must be present in" can be used for eligible employee categories—additionalinfo1 for Frontliners and additionalinfo2 for Non-Frontliners.
