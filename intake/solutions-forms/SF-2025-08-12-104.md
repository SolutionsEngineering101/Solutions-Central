## Solution Given
Please find our observations and rationale regarding the request for a customized point redemption nudge:
The client is billed at the time of point allocation, and as of yesterday, approximately 5,289 employees hold points, with a total balance of ~₹40 lakh.
There are already four system-generated nudges configured for point redemption, which are sent on the 10th, 14th, 28th, and 29th of each month. These are generic and recurring notifications applicable to users with points balance.
Based on this existing setup, the client’s employees will receive six nudges between now and September 15, 2025.
Given the frequency and existing coverage of these communications, introducing an additional customized nudge is not recommended. Moreover, the request specifies inclusion of a fixed date in the message, which would render the nudge non-reusable and limit its effectiveness beyond this specific instance.
Additionally, as we are currently in the middle of an active sprint, planning for this development in the next sprint would not be prudent.
In light of these considerations, we are unable to accommodate this request from the client.
