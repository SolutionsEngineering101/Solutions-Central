## Solution Given
Please find the responses inline:
1. Facility for nudges and escalation of never nominated associates, non-participating R&R nominators to next level. 
The built-in nudges and notifications are typically delivered via email, bell notifications and Microsoft Teams notifications. While several standard nudges are already available, we can also create customized nudges to cater to specific client use cases. The platform also features Manager Insights—a dashboard providing managers with detailed visibility into their teams' recognition patterns, enabling them to identify engagement gaps, celebrate top contributors, and reinforce a culture of appreciation. Additionally, escalation mechanisms can be developed as a customized offering to notify the next level of managers or HR teams, encouraging broader participation and program effectiveness. 

2. Elaborate on Auto generation capability of citation based on themes.
Currently, the platform supports templatized appreciation messages that can be tailored to each client’s tone and recognition culture. These templates ensure consistency while allowing some customization. The capability to auto-generate citations based on predefined themes or recognition categories (e.g., teamwork, innovation, customer service) is part of our product roadmap, expected by year-end 2025. This feature aims to streamline the nomination process by helping users quickly generate meaningful messages aligned with organizational values.

3. Does the tool allow creation of custom reports using specific attributes by drag/drop?
The platform currently offers 10+ commonly used reports accessible via the dashboard, each equipped with extensive filtering options (e.g., by department, award type, date range, etc.). In addition to these standard reports, custom reports can also be created upon request and configured for automated delivery via email at defined intervals (e.g., weekly, monthly). This provides flexibility for clients who need specific insights tailored to their organizational structure or KPIs.

4. Platform on which the application is based on ?, is the code used open source - Who pays for the platform?
Vantage Circle is a proprietary, cloud-based employee platform hosted on DigitalOcean servers. The platform is developed using a modern technology stack that includes Scala for backend development, utilizing the Play Framework, with GraphQL for the API layer. The frontend is built using Angular 15, PHP, and TypeScript to deliver a seamless and responsive user experience. The application is not open source, and all code is maintained by Vantage Circle's internal engineering team, ensuring full control over development, security, and ongoing maintenance.
The platform follows a SaaS (Software as a Service) model. It is funded by client organizations through subscription or licensing fees based on the modules they choose to implement (e.g. Recognition,Rewards, Employee Benefits, Wellness, Surveys). Employees do not pay for the use of the platform.

5. What kind of data interfaces are possible? The type of reporting functionality suggested that it would need some API/ integration with company's HRMS tools.

Our platform supports integration with various HRMS via API, ensuring consistent employee data across all entities. In addition, we integrate with collaboration tools like Microsoft Teams to enable seamless recognition sharing and notifications.

6. Can we build linkage to any other tools/ Data repositories? Ex - if we want your tool to engage with leadership stakeholders using some data points (ex - project margins, customer complaints, internal and external customer satisfaction are those feasible?)

To support advanced reporting and analytics, we offer a Data Transfer API, which allows clients to extract reports and utilize them in any data visualization or business intelligence tool of their choice (e.g., Power BI, Tableau) giving organizations full control over how they consume and analyze recognition data. This also helps leadership correlate R&R activity with broader business performance when analyzed alongside other internal metrics.

7. List of OOTB and different customized features till date for each implementation till date.
Our platform offers a comprehensive and flexible recognition ecosystem designed to support diverse organizational needs across geographies.
Key features include:
Profile Badges: Recognize specific employee traits, skills, or actions with badges that enhance employee profiles and promote organizational values.
Manager Gifting: Enable managers to directly appreciate and reward their team members with customized gifts, creating a more personal and meaningful recognition experience.
Multiple Wallets: Support multiple reward wallets for different use cases—such as allowances, festive gifting, work from home benefits, etc., allowing for clear segregation and control of recognition funds.
Long Service Awards: Offer a complete long service awards module, allowing organizations to celebrate employee milestones with customized messages, gifts, experiences and our signature Service Yearbook.
SOLI Matrix (Standard of Living Index): Ensure equitable and fair rewarding across countries using our SOLI-based model, which adjusts reward values based on local cost of living—ensuring fairness in global recognition programs.
Our recognition workflows are fully customizable, supporting up to four levels of approvals, to accommodate organizational hierarchies and compliance needs. Panel Awards with voting-based workflows, ideal for quarterly or annual recognitions that require group-based decision making.
The platform also includes an extensive global rewards catalog, featuring Gift cards, merchandise, and experiences. Amazon integration for the USA and Canada, allowing recipients to redeem points directly on Amazon within our platform for a wide selection of products.
The platform offers deep integration with the Microsoft 365 ecosystem, enabling seamless user experiences within tools like Microsoft Teams and Outlook. Through this integration, employees can send and receive recognitions, and engage with peer activity—all without leaving their Microsoft 365 environment.
Additionally, the Vantage Recognition Agent for Microsoft 365 Copilot enhances productivity and engagement by leveraging AI-powered capabilities for recognition.
