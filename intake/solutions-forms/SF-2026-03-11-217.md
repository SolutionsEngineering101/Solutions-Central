# Solution Request #217 - White Rivers Media
**Date:** 2026-03-11  |  **Status:** To Product Closed  |  **Complexity:** Low

## Brief
The client is looking for an option on the platform that allows colleagues to create posts to share their thoughts, achievements, project updates, team activities, etc., similar to a wall or feed, somewhat like Facebook. This feature would be in addition to the existing badges and awards functionality.
According to the client, a majority of their employees are below 30 years of age, and introducing such an interactive feature could encourage greater engagement on the platform. The objective is to increase user participation. Younger users are highly accustomed to platforms such as Instagram, Facebook, and LinkedIn, where the feed is the central interaction layer.

## Problem Description
The client is looking for an option on the platform that allows colleagues to create posts to share their thoughts, achievements, project updates, team activities, etc., similar to a wall or feed, somewhat like Facebook. This feature would be in addition to the existing badges and awards functionality.
According to the client, a majority of their employees are below 30 years of age, and introducing such an interactive feature could encourage greater engagement on the platform. ## Solution Given
Currently, this functionality is not available on the platform outside of the existing badges and awards features.
However, the Product team is working on a Community feature aimed at improving engagement and interaction among employees.
@Shasanka, to review this requirement and confirm whether the upcoming Community feature would align with or support the use case described by the client?
