## Solution Given
We consulted with the tech team and confirmed that the certificates are generated dynamically and are not stored within the system. As a result, it's not possible to provide them through any API.
Regarding the dummy account, it's been confirmed that only a valid email address is required. However, user creation on Vantage Circle is not necessary, so this will not affect any workflows or visibility within the platform.
Therefore, the most effective approach would be to ask the client to create a dedicated email address specifically for use in the CC matrix.
