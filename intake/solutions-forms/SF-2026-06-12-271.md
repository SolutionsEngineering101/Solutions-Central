# Solution Request #271 - PhonePe Ltd.
**Date:** 2026-06-12  |  **Status:** Open  |  **Complexity:** Medium

## Subject
Solution Request : 271271 : PhonePe Convenience Charge Removal

## Brief
Addition of point purchase system for PhonePe employees without any additional surcharge amount to the employees, point purchase should be on face value. 
For eg: INR 100 : 100 point

## Problem Description
Essential for completing the milestone awards as company wants employees to pay the remaining amount to buy the required items as per their needs.

## Solution Given
the convenience fee can be removed for domestic transactions, enabling employees to purchase points at the exact face value amount.
