# Solution Request #43 - LTTS
**Date:** 2025-06-11  |  **Status:** Rejected  |  **Complexity:** High Complexity

## Subject
Solution Request Milestone Template and Profile Picture

## Brief
Creating a Milestone Template

## Problem Description
Sending a milestone template at the start of the month- creating the PDF of all the employees who have completed a milestone during the last Month. The sample Template has been shared over the email. The template is shared with he BUHR & explore possibility of posting it in Announcement section.

## Solution Given
Regarding system-generated templates for milestone celebrations, the most practical approach would be to design a standardized PDF format with support from the Design Team—similar to the initiative we started for TATA Motors. As previously discussed, this involves several complexities that make it challenging to implement at this stage:
Issues with handling profile pictures(size, non availability and privacy issues)
Difficulties in standardizing template design, coding, and sizing(text and image)
Inability to automatically publish these through the HR announcements feature
As an alternative, the @Product Vantagecircle may consider introducing a separate "Socials Board" specifically for such posts and announcements.

## Remarks
Alternate for a Social Board or downloadable pdf suggested to Product
