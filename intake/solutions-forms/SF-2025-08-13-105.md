# Solution Request #105 - Muthoot Capital Services Limited
**Date:** 2025-08-13  |  **Status:** Solution Given Closed  |  **Complexity:** Medium Complexity

## Subject
Solution Request 112105::Workflow feasibility assessment with the employee structure

## Brief
Workflow feasibility assessment with the employee structure

## Problem Description
MCSL’s employee data contains multiple hierarchical levels, such as business lines, departments, and more. To accurately identify nominees within the platform using this multi-level data, we need to conduct a detailed assessment to determine the mandatory data points that must be obtained from the MCSL team.

## Solution Given
Quota concept can be assigned to the nominator at the award level. Once the quota is fully utilized, the nominator will be restricted from submitting further nominations within the specified timeframe, depending on the award type.
Example:
For the award “Star BDO of the Month,” which is a monthly award and can only be nominated by a single person across the organization in that month, a quota of 1 can be assigned to the nominator for this award. This means the nominator can only nominate one employee for this award in that month.
