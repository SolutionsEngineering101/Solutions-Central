## Subject
Solution Request 109102::Feasibility Check and Workaround Suggestions for Coforge’s Specific Queries

## Brief
Client has some specific queries which we need to check and provide the solutions if the feasibility is there. If not possible can we suggest some suitable workarounds for the same.

## Problem Description
Please review the following requirements from a prospect and advise on feasibility, possible approaches, and any dependencies:

1. Award Winner Presentation Slides

- Create presentation slides for award winners that can be used in town halls and manager meetings.

- Investigate the possibility of generating automated PowerPoint presentations for quarterly award announcements.

2. Panel Approval Process – Customization Options

- Ability to configure multiple panel members (3–5 approvers) for award approval workflows.

- Panel members should have commenting capabilities on submissions.

- Option to trigger interview initiation within the approval process.

3. Contractor Management for Awards

Special handling for ~400 contractors who are eligible for awards but should not have platform access.

Award notifications for these contractors should be sent to their vendors instead of directly to the contractors.

## Solution Given
Response from Solutions team are in blue font.                                      1. Award Winner Presentation Slides
This feature is currently not available. However, a similar capability powered by AI is already part of our  roadmap.
2. Panel Approval Process – Customization Options
- Ability to configure multiple panel members (3–5 approvers) for award approval workflows.
Yes, this is feasible. The panel award option in the HR dashboard allows configuration of multiple approvers.
- Panel members should have commenting capabilities on submissions.
Yes, panel members can add comments on the platform during the award announcement process.
- Option to trigger interview initiation within the approval process.
This functionality is not available, as it falls outside the scope of our platform.
3. Contractor Management for Awards
Special handling for ~400 contractors who are eligible for awards but should not have platform access. Award notifications for these contractors should be sent to their vendors instead of directly to the contractors.
To proceed, could you please clarify how the client would like to manage R&R for these contractors? Are they expecting monetary awards, non-monetary recognition, certificates, or a combination?
