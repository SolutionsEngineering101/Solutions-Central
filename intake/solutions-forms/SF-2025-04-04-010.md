# Solution Request #10 - Tata Motors
**Date:** 2025-04-04  |  **Status:** Rejected  |  **Complexity:** High Complexity

## Subject
Request for Feasibility, Timelines, and Costs – Automated Award Mailer through Accolades Portal

Request for Feasibility, Timelines, and Costs – Accolades Award Mailers and Reward Portal

## Brief
Automate the award mailer process for Platinum, Gold, Silver, and Bronze awards by integrating a backend system that populates award details into predefined templates (attached in the mail) and dispatches the mailers to designated SPOCs.

## Problem Description
Backend Automation
•	Extract award details (project names, winners, award levels) from the backend database.
•	Populate extracted details into predefined email- certificate templates (attached).
•	Validate data accuracy before dispatch.
Mailer Distribution
•	Map and send emails to designated SPOCs as per the award type.
•	Ensure restricted access to mailers based on the distribution matrix.
•	Maintain an audit trail for mail dispatch (timestamp, recipient, status, etc.).

## Solution Given
SPOC details can be added to additional info of respective employees to send CC email notification to SPOC for the awards won by those employees.

Request for development of an automated template emailer for winners was rejected.
