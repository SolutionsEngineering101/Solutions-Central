## Problem Description
1. The Client NuSummit wants the Service Yearbook feature to be department-specific, where the " Share Your Thoughts " email trigger should go only to the users of the specific department. 
2. The client has requested that the upcoming anniversary tab in that case should show the users of only the specific department
3. The client wants to include specific email id's (example - CEO, CFO) for specific milestone years so that the Share your thoughts email gets triggered to them apart from the regular trigger as per the default configuration. Similarly, they would also want to exclude specific email IDs from the standard anniversary-year default configuration email triggers.

## Solution Given
Department-Specific “Share Your Thoughts” Email Trigger : The “Share Your Thoughts” email can be configured to trigger only for users belonging to the same department as the anniversary employee.
Regarding the requirement for the congratulatory email to be triggered to both the employee and their department colleagues. Currently, we do not have a provision to share the exact same congratulatory email received by the anniversary employee with department members. However, we have previously implemented a similar customisation for Wipro, where users who submitted “Share Your Thoughts” comments were CC’d in the communication. A similar approach can be evaluated and tested for NuSummit as well.
Upcoming Anniversary Tab – Department Restriction : This section is currently linked with the Global Feed filter configuration. To achieve department-wise visibility restriction in the Upcoming Anniversary tab, the client would also need to agree to implementing department-based feed restrictions at the global feed level (assuming the secondary filter remains unchanged as the department filter itself).
This configuration can be managed from the Site Admin panel, and the TechOps team can assist further with the setup and guidance if required.
Specific Email IDs for Milestone Years : Currently, year specific recipient configuration is not supported. However, the requested email IDs (for example, CEO, CFO, etc.) can be configured as additional recipients across all milestone years under the existing setup.
