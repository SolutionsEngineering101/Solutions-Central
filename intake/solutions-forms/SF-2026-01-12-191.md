# Solution Request #191 - Muthoot Capital Services Limited
**Date:** 2026-01-12  |  **Status:** Rejected  |  **Complexity:** Low

## Subject
Feasibility Check: Customisation of Feed Post | Muthoot Capital

## Brief
Utilising the standard monetary awards with the associated workflow, Muthoot Capital team wants to remove the nominator's name on the feed post generated on the platform.

## Problem Description
Muthoot Capital team wants to remove the nominator's name from the feed post generated on the platform. Since their Head BIS will be the person who will be nominating the deserved employees for most of the monetary awards on behalf of the HODs, they do not want the name of the Head BIS to be appearing on the feed as the giver of the awards.

## Solution Given
The request to hide the nominator’s name from feed posts for Muthoot Capital is not feasible within standard product capabilities, as nominator details are deeply integrated across feed posts, award history, and reporting views, and removing them would create data inconsistencies.
