## Brief

## Problem Description
Category option in Monetary & Non-Monetary doesn't work as desired, current scheme is hybrid where somw changes can be done from backend, some from the frontend. Giving a poor experience.

## Solution Given
Category deletion was intentionally restricted from the dashboard to prevent accidental data loss, as categories are directly linked to awards and badges. Deleting a category could make associated awards and badges invisible on the platform, impacting the employee recognition experience.
We have noted the feedback and agree that this can be enabled as a self-serve dashboard feature with the necessary safeguards in place. The team will evaluate the feasibility and share timelines accordingly.
Until then, category deletions will continue to be managed by the TechOps team.
