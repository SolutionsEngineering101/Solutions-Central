# Solution Request #235 - Nadhashri HS
**Date:** 2026-04-13  |  **Status:** To Product Closed  |  **Complexity:** Not Set

## Brief
Daily reward budget allocation to every employee with same-day expiry (no accumulation)

## Problem Description
The client requires a change in the reward points allocation mechanism. Instead of allocating points to givers on a monthly basis, they want points to be credited to every employee daily to their reward budget.

Key requirements:

Points should be allocated daily to each employee
Points must lapse if not used on the same day
No accumulation or carry-forward of unused points
System should enforce expiry automatically at end of day
