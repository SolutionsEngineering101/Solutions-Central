# Solution Request #151 - Mitsubishi Chemical America
**Date:** 2025-10-21  |  **Status:** Solution Given Closed  |  **Complexity:** Low Complexity

## Subject
Solution Request 156151 Mitsubishi Chemical America

## Brief
1.Nominator Should get award too:

As per the panel award workflow requirement, the nominator should receive a portion of the award when their nominee wins.
The incentive for the nominator will be a percentage of the award received by the awardee, typically in the range of 10–15%.

2.Award Allocation Based on Cities/Cluster of cities:

Awards will be distributed based on cities or cluster of cities, where each cluster may comprise one or more cities.
Each cluster will have a specific award designated for nominations.
A dedicated panel committee responsible for voting the nominations from that cluster.

3.Manual Award Distribution via CSV (Without Feed Posting):

If the award process is to be handled outside the system feed (i.e., not posting results or announcements on the internal feed), but awards/points still need to be allocated:

The panel nomination process can be conducted within the system.

The final award results can be compiled externally and distributed manually.

Award points can then be uploaded and granted through a manual CSV upload, following the format and process supported by the system’s bulk award upload functionality.

Ensuring the CSV contains all required fields.

Awards for both the nominee and the nominator are included with appropriate point values based on the defined percentage split.

## Solution Given
Clarifications Required
Award Recipient Confirmation
The form refers to the nominator receiving a percentage of the award. However, during our call, it was mentioned this might be the panelist instead. Could you please confirm which role is intended to receive the incentive?
City/Cluster Data Availability
Are user city or cluster fields already being captured or passed into the system? It would be helpful to know the employee details being captured.
Could you confirm the communication preferences?
Why does the client not wish to announce winners on the platform? Should award results not be posted et all on the internal Feed?
Should winners and nominators receive congratulatory emails or notifications?

Following our discussion yesterday, we had an internal review today and propose the following solution for implementing Panel Awards:
Business Division will be mapped to the Department column in the employee details.
City will be mapped to the Entity column.
Separate Panel Awards will be created for each designated Division and City (or grouped region), each with its own set of Panelists and a unique Winner Selector as defined by the respective HRBP.
Panel Award names can include identifiers (e.g., Division + City or Region) to support easier tracking and maintenance via the dashboard.
Clusters of cities can be grouped under regional names (e.g., Central, North) based on client preferences.
The Award Description can include key details for quick reference.
Awards will be announced in line with the respective Panel Award timelines.
To facilitate awarding nominators of the winners, a separate CSV upload can be done via the dashboard.
