# Solution Request #233 - AM Green Energy
**Date:** 2026-04-06  |  **Status:** Solution Given Closed  |  **Complexity:** Not Set

## Brief
Enabling the bell notification for 2nd level approval

## Problem Description
Currently, we observe that for the 1st level approver, both email and bell notifications are enabled. However, for the 2nd level approver, only email notifications are available, and the bell notification is not enabled.
Ideally, when a 2nd level approver logs into the platform, they should be able to see a bell notification indicating that a new nomination is pending for their approval. This is important because they may not check the "To-Do" list every time they log in.
