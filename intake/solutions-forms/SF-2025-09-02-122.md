# Solution Request #122 - LTTS
**Date:** 2025-09-02  |  **Status:** Open  |  **Complexity:** Low complexity

## Subject
Solution Request 129122 LTTS program email ID

## Brief
all email for LTTS should be triggered from info@ltts.com or any other email like roar@ltts.com instead of info@vantagecircle.com

## Problem Description
all email for LTTS should be triggered from info@ltts.com or any other email like roar@ltts.com instead of info@vantagecircle.com

## Solution Given
Our Understanding of the Requirement: LTTS would like to customize the sender email address used in the R&R program, changing it from info@vantagecircle.com to something that better reflects their branding, such as info@ltts.com or roar@ltts.com.

Proposed Solution: While it is technically possible to send emails from @ltts.com, we do not recommend this approach. Routing through a client domain requires additional configuration on both sides, ongoing maintenance, and introduces authentication, security, and scalability challenges if multiple clients request the same.
Recommended Approach: As an alternative, we can customize the email prefix while retaining our verified domain. For example, the sender email can be set as:
roar@vantagecircle.com or
roar-ltts@vantagecircle.com 
This ensures strong branding alignment while maintaining the security, deliverability, and stability of our email infrastructure.
Next Steps: Once confirmed, we can initiate the change, and it will take a maximum of 2 working days.
