# Solution Request #143 - Hyundai Capital Canada
**Date:** 2025-09-30  |  **Status:** To Product Closed  |  **Complexity:** Low Complexity

## Subject
Solution Request 148143 Annoucements

## Brief
Request to Include Full Announcement in Triggered Emails

## Problem Description
Hyundai Capital Canada has requested that the entire announcement content be included directly within the email that gets triggered when an announcement is made.

This means that, instead of just a link or partial text, the complete announcement message should appear in the triggered email for recipients.

Kindly confirm if this can be accommodated, and if so, please let us know the next steps required to enable it.

## Solution Given
Our Understanding:
The client has requested that the entire announcement content be visible within the email itself, rather than just a link or a brief text.
Current Progress:
We have already incorporated the Title of the announcement in the email subject line.
Work is currently in progress to include the full announcement in the body of the email.
Contextual Feedback:
While the initial approach was to use the announcement as a means to pull users to the platform, it has been highlighted that there is a need for more context within the email itself to make the announcements more useful and actionable.
Next Steps:
For announcements containing images, PDFs, or videos, we will revisit and provide an update post the holiday break.

Reply 2: For announcements that include images, PDFs, or videos, work is currently underway to support these media types as email attachments. This enhancement is expected to be completed by the end of October.
