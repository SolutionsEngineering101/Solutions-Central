## Problem Description
We have received a requirement related to Panel Awards from one of our clients, Omron (1775). Kindly review the feasibility and confirm.

Requirement:

The client would like the winners of the Panel Awards to be displayed on the platform in a manner similar to the existing Leaderboard section. Currently, this activity is being managed manually by the HR POC and shared with leadership and employees.
The request is to automate this process within the platform, which is expected to enhance employee engagement and overall platform activity.

## Solution Given
The proposal is to use the Award Winner Digest Email feature to automate sharing of panel award results. This email sends a PDF listing department‑wise award winners for a selected time period (e.g., monthly), with the frequency set at the company level. Only users mapped under the HOD field in the Admin Dashboard receive it, and each recipient gets a personalized PDF showing award winners only for the departments they oversee. For this requirement, HR can be added as an HOD so they also receive the consolidated award details.
