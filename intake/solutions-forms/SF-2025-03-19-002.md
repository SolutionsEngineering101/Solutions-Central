# Solution Request #2 - Servion
**Date:** 2025-03-19  |  **Status:** Solution Given Closed  |  **Complexity:** Low Complexity

## Subject
Company Branded Merchandise Only Option for Redemption

## Brief
Customization in Redmeption section, need only Merchandise with own vendor

## Problem Description
1. Gift Cards and Experiences sections should be disabled on the Redemption page.
2. Only their company-branded merchandise should be visible under the Merchandise section.
3. They already have a vendor supplying these branded items, so when someone redeems the item, the HR will procure the same from the vendor and hand them to the employees in person.
4. However, they require an email notification to be triggered to the HR Team whenever an employee redeems their points for company-branded merchandise.

## Solution Given
1. Disabling Gift Cards and Experiences: Our Tech-Ops team can hide these sections, leaving only Merchandise visible on the Redemption page.
2. Showing Only Company-Branded Merchandise: The Merchandise section can display just branded items, with @Udayan Bordoloi setting up the deals.
3. Emailing the HR Team: We can add an HR group email to vendor redemption reports, sent daily, weekly, or monthly.
