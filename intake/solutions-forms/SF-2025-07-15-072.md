## Solution Given
Thank you for submitting the solutions form, but we need more clarity on what you are seeking.

Based on our discussion, please find the answers below:

Budget Reports: There are various reports available on the dashboard that HR admin can view and download:
Purchased Budget Report
Detailed overview of budget purchase transactions, country-wise.

Budget Allocation Report
Detailed overview of reward budgets allocated to budget holders.

Budget Utilization Report
Detailed overview of reward budgets spent by budget holders.

Budget balance of Managers: HR admin can view the budget balance of the Managers (only those who have direct reportees) from the Budget section in the Vantage Recognition module of the Dashboard.

Point balance of end users: HR admin cannot see the point balance of end users. This is their account information. HR admin can extract details on how many points were awarded to which users using the Employee Points Report.

Gift Card available: This information can be viewed from the platform frontend or the support team can help with a list of gift cards available in various countries.

SOLI: Our rewarding system is based on the SOLI (Standard of Living Index) framework to help companies recognize global employees. It ensures equal and fair regulation of reward points against currencies for employees across the globe. The SOLI framework goes beyond the currency conversion rates (which change frequently). It automatically adjusts the monetary rewards as per the standard of living of the country. This makes the regulation of reward points fair and equal.
