## Subject
Solution request 190187 ::Need for Export and Pagination Feature in Points Statement

## Brief
Need for Export and Pagination Feature in Points Statement

## Problem Description
Currently, there is no feature available to export the points statement from the Vantage Circle account, nor is there a pagination option to view the statement in an organised manner. If an export feature is enabled, it would significantly simplify the process for support representatives to identify discrepancies in the points statement and also help users review their points easily, especially in cases of impending expiry or mismatches. Overall, this enhancement would reduce the time and effort required to investigate points discrepancy issues and improve efficiency for both users and support teams.
In the absence of this feature, the points statement must be manually copied from the Site Admin, organised in an Excel sheet, and then revalidated, which is time-consuming, effort-intensive, and carries a risk of missing line items.
Overall, the time required to investigate points discrepancy issues is significantly reduced, enabling faster and more efficient resolution.

## Solution Given
The preview functionality for employee details upload will be picked in the upcoming sprint.Smart validations are already supported, and a preview option for Employee Uploads will be added in an upcoming sprint. Existing validation mechanisms also auto-correct common formatting issues and provide detailed error reports for skipped rows. However, implementing a two-step approval for employee uploads would require significant architectural changes and may disrupt existing client workflows, so no immediate action is planned on that front.
