# Solution Request #96 - ICICI Lombard
**Date:** 2025-08-05  |  **Status:** Solution Given Closed  |  **Complexity:** Medium Complex

## Subject
Solution Request 10396::ICICI Lombard – Profile & Platform Customization Requests

## Brief
1. Removal of 'Receive occasional mail about special offers (not more than once a week).' and 'Require my consent and approval before any recognition(s) are published on the feed in the platform' from My Profile section under Edit profile
2. Removal of FAQ section from platform that appears on clicking profile
3. Customization of Manager Guide based on client guidelines

These are for the client ICICI Lombard

## Problem Description
1. Removal of 'Receive occasional mail about special offers (not more than once a week).' and 'Require my consent and approval before any recognition(s) are published on the feed in the platform' from My Profile section under Edit profile
2. Removal of FAQ section from platform that appears on clicking profile
3. Customization of Manager Guide based on client guidelines

## Solution Given
We have thoroughly reviewed the requirements and consulted with the Tech-Ops team. They have confirmed that removing the two options from the 'My Profile' section and the FAQ section from the user profile is technically feasible and can be implemented for the client without affecting the overall platform functionality. However, the request to customize the Manager Guide would require changes to a core, standardized part of the platform. Implementing this could impact product consistency and long-term maintainability across clients, so it is currently out of scope and not recommended.
