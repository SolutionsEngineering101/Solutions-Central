# Solution Request #218 - Decera Clinical
**Date:** 2026-03-11  |  **Status:** Solution Given Closed  |  **Complexity:** Medium

## Subject
Solution Request222218::Alignment Required::Department Field Usage Impacting Platform Features (Decera Clinical)

## Brief
Currently, we are populating Job Titles under the Department field in the employee details because the client wanted the job titles to appear under the Name section in the user profile.

## Problem Description
This approach will impact the Insights and the filters in the Admin Dashboard, as most of the filters are department-specific and rely on the Department field being used correctly.

To resolve this, we should use one of the Additional Info fields to store the Job Titles and map it from the backend so that it continues to appear in the user profile. Once this is done, we can revert the Department column back to its original department data to ensure that the Insights and Admin Dashboard filters function correctly.

## Solution Given
Discussion Summary
Currently, the Job Title/Designation has been mapped under the Department field to display the designation below the employee name in the profile.
Since Department is a core product field used for Insights segmentation, reporting, dashboard filters, and recognition analytics, using it for Job Title may lead to inconsistencies in platform functionality.
Agreed Corrective Approach
Store Job Titles/designation in the Additional Info1 field in the employee data.
Revert the Department column back to the correct department data,
