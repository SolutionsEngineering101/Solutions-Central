## Subject
Solution Request 7771 typical gift value or point range

## Brief
What is the typical gift value or point range available?

## Problem Description
We received an RFP from Xactly where this question was asked by the prospect.

## Solution Given
Gift values and point ranges vary based on reward type and client configuration. Monetary gift cards and merchandise typically start at $5 (or the local currency equivalent) and can exceed $1,000. Points-based rewards are fully customizable by the client, with common configurations ranging from 0 to over 100,000 points, usually mapped to local currency or SOLI-adjusted equivalents. Experiential and premium rewards are available upon specific company requests. Award values are also client-specific, defined in fixed denominations and mapped to local currencies for each country.
