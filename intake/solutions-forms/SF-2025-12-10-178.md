## Brief
Request to create a one time, limited duration Amazon based catalogue for a specific group of MPAC employees, separate from milestone logic.

## Problem Description
MPAC would like to open a one time gift catalogue for a defined list of 150 to 200 employees who are not part of their milestone program. Each employee will receive a gift within a consistent price range of approximately 75 to 100 dollars. The catalogue window needs to remain open for 20 to 30 days, targeted for January or February.

In UAT, only two catalogue options are currently visible. For this use case, we need the ability to create an additional standalone catalogue that can be named for MPAC, restricted to the specified price range, and sourced through Amazon. Eligibility must be based on a custom user list provided by the client.

Key actions required:
• Confirm capability to add an additional catalogue type
• Enable Amazon sourcing for this catalogue
• Confirm configuration steps to apply a narrow price range
• Ensure the catalogue can be activated and deactivated for a fixed 20 to 30 day period
• Provide any constraints or timelines Product needs us to communicate to MPAC

Once enabled, I will configure and test this catalogue in UAT and send it to the client for review before deployment to production.
