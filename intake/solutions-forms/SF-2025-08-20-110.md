# Solution Request #110 - Xactly (Prospect)
**Date:** 2025-08-20  |  **Status:** Solution Given Closed  |  **Complexity:** Low Complexity

## Subject
Urgent: Clarification Needed for Snappy Client - Swag Vendor Process & Global Logistics

## Brief
Global Company Branded Swag

## Problem Description
Hi Hemanga and team,

I need your urgent help with a potential client, following an RFP submission and presentation. We have a potential client who currently uses Snappy for gifts and swag but is looking to consolidate all their recognition programs onto one platform and has shortlisted Vantage Circle. This is a global company with employees in the US, Canada, UK, Japan, and over 50% of their population in India, which is a key consideration.

Here are the critical points:

Current Client Challenge: They manually use Snappy for anniversary and gift cards, but it's not a full-service, points-based platform that meets their needs. They want to switch to a single, consolidated platform.

Key Client Requirement: They need a solution that can handle custom, branded swag with reliable shipping, especially for their large employee base in India.

RFP question with our submitted responses:

"1. Describe opportunities to provide company branded swag items (either in specific catalogs or as a Swag store).  
Response: Vantage Recognition has the ability to offer a selection of customized gifts and exclusive Swag items. We work with the client’s chosen vendor to support branding requirements. The vendor manages the production and design aspects, while we facilitate the integration of these products into the rewards catalog for easy redemption. 
2. Can we create branded logos specifically for a 10th work anniversary or 20th anniversary and add them to a custom catalog (as a specific example use case)? 
Response: Yes, our merchandise partners can create custom inventory for specific occasions. We'll collaborate closely with your team to finalize designs and product selections before hosting these items in your redemption catalog. For service anniversaries, a popular approach involves symbolic awards with custom logos. Sample catalogs for these items are available upon request." 

Based on our responses, they have followed up with a critical question that I need to answer clearly:

"Am I reading it correctly that you do not have a relationship with a swag vendor (we would need to find/use our own and pay for them separately?). If we use our own vendor, how does this 'facilitation' process work and is there a Vantage Circle fee associated with this step?"

Information I Need from You
To answer this question and protect us in the deal, I need:

1.Process Details: How exactly do we "facilitate" the process when a client uses their own vendor? Can you provide a step-by-step breakdown of how this would work?

2. Our Current In-House Vendor(s): What is the pulse on the roll-out of our current partnership/integration with Snappy/Covver? Can you elaborate on what this process would look like for a company who requires branded merchandise in India (Accolades) and NA+?

3. Global Logistics: How would the rollout of branded merchandise and shipping work for each country, specifically for the employee population in India and across the US, Canada, UK, and Japan?

4. Associated Fees: Is there a specific fee associated with this facilitation process?

I need to respond to the client within the next 10 hours. Any guidance on these items would be a huge help, and I'm available for a quick 15-minute huddle before 12:00 PM IST.

## Solution Given
Your answers to the RFP questions are correct; however, please note that the details shared apply only to India.
The responses to the follow-up questions below provide further clarity on the specific processes involved:
Process Details: How exactly do we "facilitate" the process when a client uses their own vendor? Can you provide a step-by-step breakdown of how this would work? If a client has a preferred vendor, we support them through the following process (available only in India):
The client's vendor is onboarded on mutually agreed business terms.
The client's vendor shares the SKUs of their merchandise items requested by the client.
We add these items to our merchandise catalog on the platform.
Employees can place orders through this catalog as they normally would.
We then coordinate order fulfillment offline directly with the client’s vendor.
If the client does not have a vendor, we also have in-house vendor options available to support their needs. 

Our Current In-House Vendor(s): What is the pulse on the roll-out of our current partnership/integration with Snappy/Covver? Can you elaborate on what this process would look like for a company who requires branded merchandise in India (Accolades) and NA+? We are currently in the process of integrating with Snappy/Covver. This integration is still underway, with an estimated timeline of 3–4 more months for completion. The contract signing is still not completed. Once the integration is live, we will be able to offer branded merchandise through our merchandise catalog in all key regions — including the US, Canada, UK, India, and Japan — enabling consistent global support for branded swag. However, this catalog will not be available for the Milestone Catalog (i.e., it cannot be used for items such as plaques or trophies)

Global Logistics: How would the rollout of branded merchandise and shipping work for each country, specifically for the employee population in India and across the US, Canada, UK, and Japan? At present, our branded merchandise capabilities are limited to India through our existing vendors. We do not currently have vendor partnerships outside of India, which means we are unable to support branded swag merchandise orders in the US, Canada, UK, or Japan at this time. However, this gap will be addressed once the Snappy/Covver integration is completed.

Associated Fees: Is there a specific fee associated with this facilitation process? Currently, we do not charge any additional fees for this facilitation process when using the systems already in place.  The client's vendor has to be onboarded on our platform through a proper contract between the Vendor and Vantage Circle covering aspects of listing, update, delivery, pricing, and payment terms. 

Please note: While we are working on expanding global coverage for branded swag items through an upcoming integration with Snappy/Covver (expected in 3–4 months), this will not include items like plaques or trophies, which will continue to be supported only in India.
