# Solution Request #234 - Tata Play
**Date:** 2026-04-07  |  **Status:** Unknown  |  **Complexity:** Not Set

## Brief
Automation of SPOT of the month award (Standard Award)- Tata Play

## Problem Description
Currently, for Tata Play, users directly nominate colleagues for the SPOT Award through the platform. In the subsequent month, the SPOT Award winners need to be considered for the “SPOT of the Month” award.
At present, this process requires significant manual intervention. The Vantage Circle team logs into the BigStage@tataplay.com admin account and manually performs the following steps each month:
Access the list of SPOT Award winners from the previous month.
Select each winner individually.
Choose the SPOT of the Month award.
Enter the citation message along with nominator details in the required format.
Submit the nomination for approval.
We would like to request a product enhancement to automate this workflow. The client already has a similar automated process in place for Panel Awards – Progressive Panel Award, where winners of SPOT of the Month are automatically nominated by the system. Implementing a similar automation for the SPOT to SPOT of the Month workflow will significantly reduce manual effort, ensure consistency, and improve operational efficiency.
