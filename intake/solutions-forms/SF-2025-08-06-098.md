## Subject
Solution Request 10598::Custom Data Visibility Control Based on User Role Attributes (Country, Department, Legal Entity)

## Brief
Custom Data Visibility Control Based on User Role Attributes (Country, Department, Legal Entity)

## Problem Description
The client has requested enhancements in the platform’s user role structure to enable data visibility restrictions based on country, department, and legal entity.

Currently, the HR Admin role is defined at the department level, allowing visibility of data across all countries for that department. However, the client needs to restrict access further:

Country-specific HR managers should only see data related to their own country and not across other countries.

Leaders and managers should have access limited to specific combinations of country and department.

Key actions requested:

Introduce or configure user roles with granular access control, where visibility can be filtered by country, department, and legal entity.

Explore the possibility of either customizing existing user roles or implementing a new role matrix to meet this requirement.

Share if there are any alternative solutions or planned developments that could help address these visibility constraints.

## Solution Given
The Product Team is already working on enhancing roles and permissions within the dashboard, and similar considerations are being made for report access and visibility to align with the requirements.
