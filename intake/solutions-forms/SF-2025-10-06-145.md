# Solution Request #145 - Tech Mahindra
**Date:** 2025-10-06  |  **Status:** Open  |  **Complexity:** Low Complexity

## Subject
Solution request124117::Exploring Secure Access Options for Tech Mahindra’s Vantage Perks Platform

## Brief
As discussed in the recent conversation with the Tech Mahindra team, they would like the Vantage Perks portal access to be restricted to their company domain, with an OTP-based login sent to each user’s active company email address.

They are not open to manually uploading employee information via CSV or proceeding with a full-fledged HRIS/SSO integration.

This request is to:

1. Understand the costing involved in white-labelling the portal and implementing OTP-based login access restricted to their company domain.

2. Assess any foreseeable challenges in implementing this solution across multiple geographies where Vantage Perks is currently available — including India, the US, the UK, and Australia.

3. Evaluate how to ensure that employees can access only the deals relevant to their respective locations within the OTP-based login framework.

4. Estimate the time required to fully set up and configure the Perks platform for Tech Mahindra.
