## Solution Given
Hello @Kongkana,
Thank you for the detailed clarifications shared in the email thread below.

Regarding the request for an additional budget-utilisation email after every approval, we would like to highlight that the existing Budget Reminder email already includes budget and utilisation details. This email is triggered on the 2nd and 4th Thursdays of every month, and a sample has been attached for reference.
