# Solution Request #1 - Merit Group
**Date:** 2025-03-17  |  **Status:** Solution Given Closed  |  **Complexity:** Low Complexity

## Subject
Merit Group <> Auto Trigger of points for new joiners

## Brief
Auto Trigger of points for new joiners

## Problem Description
Any new joiner in the system from 1st April 2025 (basically post Go Live) should receive 100 points as a part of their onboarding process. CSV upload was suggested but the client wants an auto trigger feature on the platform by checking the doj of the user.

## Solution Given
1. Keep the 'Registration points' Actionable disabled and manually assign '100' points to new joiners using the CSV Awards option during the first load.
2. Afterward, the 'Registration points' Actionable can be activated. (Auto-registered users will also receive the registration points, which will be confirmed after testing)
3. Manual reconciliation of points will be required until any product enhancements are implemented in this area.
