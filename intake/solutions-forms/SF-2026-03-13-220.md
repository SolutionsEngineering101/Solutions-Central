# Solution Request #220 - ASPIRE
**Date:** 2026-03-13  |  **Status:** Solution Given Closed  |  **Complexity:** Not Set

## Brief
Request to add a tax disclaimer in reward point credit email communications (Monetary Awards, Milestone Awards, and CSV Awards) as per client requirement. This will ensure users are informed that reward points credited under the program are considered taxable income and applicable TDS will be deducted through payroll as per the Income Tax Act, 1961.

## Problem Description
The client has requested the inclusion of a tax disclaimer in all email communications where reward points are credited to users. This applies specifically to Monetary Awards, Milestone Awards, and CSV Awards email templates.

The disclaimer should inform users that reward points credited under the reward program are considered taxable income under the Income Tax Act, 1961, and that applicable TDS will be deducted through payroll in the month in which the taxable value is accounted.

Key Actions Required:

Update the email communication templates for Monetary Awards, Milestone Awards, and CSV Awards.

Add the following disclaimer below the email signature in the mailer content as per the client’s request:

“Reward points credited under the reward program are considered as taxable income under the Income Tax Act, 1961. Applicable TDS will be deducted through payroll in the month in which the taxable value is accounted.”

Ensure the disclaimer is included in all future communications where reward points are credited to users for this client.
