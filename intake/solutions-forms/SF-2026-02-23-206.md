# Solution Request #206 - Amdocs
**Date:** 2026-02-23  |  **Status:** Open  |  **Complexity:** Low Complexity

## Subject
Solution Request 210206 Amdocs Budgets/Wallets iCare-a-Gift sync

## Brief
Amdocs wants to create managers wallet

## Problem Description
Amdocs is looking to give managers rights to give points on the platform by enabling the wallets for them.

## Solution Given
Our Understanding:
Based on the slide shared earlier and our discussion with the client, we understand that Amdocs manages multiple budgets for its recognition programs:
Division Budget – Controlled by COE members within each division and used to award employees with points, gifts, and vouchers.
Manager Gifting Budget – A separate budget allocated to managers for one-to-one gifting of points, physical gifts, and vouchers.
Peer-to-Peer Rewards Budget – Managed through iCare, which requires a complete two-way sync to be integrated into a-gift.
Amdocs would like to explore options to consolidate these budgets within the a-gift (Vantage Circle) platform without requiring any additional development, leveraging existing platform features.
