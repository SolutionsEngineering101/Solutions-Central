## Problem Description
We have received two new requirements from our newly onboarded client, White Rivers Media.
Please find the details below:
Inclusion of a “Share Your Thoughts” message in the WhatsApp notifications to encourage greater user participation.
Clarification on whether the WhatsApp sender name can be customized to reflect either the company name (WRM) or the program name (ShineOn).

## Solution Given
Our understanding: The client would like to send a “Share Your Thought” notification to users via WhatsApp to increase user participation. Additionally, the client prefers that the sender name displayed to users be either the company name (WRM) or the program name (ShineOn) instead of Vantage Circle.


Considerations: While we already have an integration in place with WhatsApp for transactional messaging, implementing this request will require additional development effort and cost to incorporate this specific use case into the existing WhatsApp messaging flow.
Further, sending messages via WhatsApp requires approval of templates. Based on our previous experience, WhatsApp typically prioritizes transactional communications, and engagement-driven messages such as “Share Your Thought” may face rejection during the template approval process.
From a cost perspective, WhatsApp messaging also incurs a recurring per-message cost. This cost will be factored into the per-user fee paid by the client after calculating the average messaging cost per user, along with the additional development cost.

Recommendation: Considering the potential template approval limitations and the associated costs, we recommend that the client encourage users to install the mobile application through either monetary or non-monetary campaigns.
Once the user base has installed the application, Push Notifications and Bell Notifications can be effectively leveraged to prompt users to share their thoughts and increase overall participation.
