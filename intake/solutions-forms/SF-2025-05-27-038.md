# Solution Request #38 - Digital people/ Sartorious/ Tata tele services / MSAF
**Date:** 2025-05-27  |  **Status:** Solution Given Closed  |  **Complexity:** Medium Complexity

## Subject
Solution Request 4438 Country Budget Visibility

## Brief
Currently, the available budget in the common wallet is only visible either through reports or while adding budgets via the upload budget tab. This creates a challenge for HR SPOCs, who need to switch between tabs frequently before releasing any points through CSV uploads.

Since the common wallet is associated with a dummy user, it would be highly beneficial to display the remaining budget in these wallets—broken down by country—directly on the dashboard. This would streamline the reward process, making it faster and more efficient, while also reducing dependency on CSMs.

As most uploads happen during off-hours, CSMs are not always available to top up the wallets—an action that can currently only be done by Vantage Account Managers. This limitation creates a bottleneck, resulting in delayed point disbursement and a suboptimal experience for users.

## Problem Description
Real time budget display will help planning the upload points in a much more structured way. all the add-on request can com in early and dependencies can be reduced from the account managers end.

## Solution Given
The issue was discussed and it was agreed to show the selected country budget in the Bulk upload window under the OVerall section in the Dashboard.

Following our discussion, the plan was to display country-wise budgets on the Bulk Allocate and Bulk Upload pages in the Dashboard.
However, after consulting with the Tech team, we’ve realized this approach may not address the core issue. Your request essentially requires a user-level view of the current balances for Dummy Budget Holders.
At present, we don't have a mechanism to distinguish dummy users from real ones. That said, we are exploring the possibility of generating a report that provides a user-level view of the current balance for all users.
We’ll keep you updated as we make progress on this.


After consulting with our Tech and R&D teams, we’ve confirmed that real-time reports cannot currently be hosted on the Dashboard.
We can display country and department-level budget information within the Bulk Allocate workflow on the Dashboard.
For user-level budget availability, HR admins will need to download the Budget Report. Please note that all reports are indexed every 24 hours.
