# Solution Request #31 - Mitsogo Technologies and Antara Senior Care
**Date:** 2025-05-15  |  **Status:** Solution Given Closed  |  **Complexity:** Medium Complexity

## Subject
Removal of Panel voters/Approver details during the nomination process

## Brief
It has been requested to remove the nomination workflow page that currently displays approver details for Standard Awards and panel voter information for Panel Awards.

## Problem Description
We’ve received a few client requests to remove the page in the nomination process that displays the details of the approver (for standard awards) or panel voters (for panel awards) on the platform.

Here are the two specific requests:

Mitsogo/Hexnode Technologies:
The Mitsogo team is using Panel Awards on their platform. Currently, during the nomination process, nominators can view the list of panel voters/approvers. They have requested that this page be removed, as they do not want nominators to see this information. This is a critical requirement for them, and they may not be able to launch Panel Awards without this change.

Antara Senior Care:
Antara has auto-approved awards configured. In such cases, the system currently shows the nominator’s name as the approver, which causes confusion. They have requested to hide this detail as it doesn’t serve a meaningful purpose. This is also an important ask from the client.

## Solution Given
It is possible to skip the Approver window. Doing so will skip the CC box in that window too. 

It has been put to testing and once confirmed will be implemented for both the clients.
