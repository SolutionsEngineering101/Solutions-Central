# Solution Request #175 - phData Solutions
**Date:** 2025-12-01  |  **Status:** Solution Given Closed  |  **Complexity:** Medium complexity

## Subject
New Requirement - phData Solutions, India

## Brief
My client, phData Solutions, is currently using our Milestone Catalogue and Onboarding Kit modules. However, they have reported some issues related to stock availability information and delivery timelines. During my recent in-person meeting, the client emphasized the need for improvements, and after internal discussions, I would like to understand the feasibility of the following enhancements:
Introducing a stock visibility feature similar to common e-commerce platforms — for example, indicators such as “1 left,” “2 left,” etc.
Purpose: To give users clarity on limited stock availability and encourage timely ordering.
Implementing real-time delivery tracking — including order placement date, dispatch date, and expected delivery date.
Purpose: To provide the HR team with better visibility, as they currently have no information when employees contact them for delivery updates after placing an order.
Please check the feasibility of the above mentioned items and if they cannot be implemented, what could be offered as the best alternative.

## Problem Description
My client, phData Solutions, is currently using our Milestone Catalogue and Onboarding Kit modules. However, they have reported some issues related to stock availability information and delivery timelines. During my recent in-person meeting, the client emphasized the need for improvements, and after internal discussions, I would like to understand the feasibility of the following enhancements:
Introducing a stock visibility feature similar to common e-commerce platforms — for example, indicators such as “1 left,” “2 left,” etc.
Purpose: To give users clarity on limited stock availability and encourage timely ordering.
Implementing real-time delivery tracking — including order placement date, dispatch date, and expected delivery date.
Purpose: To provide the HR team with better visibility, as they currently have no information when employees contact them for delivery updates after placing an order.
Please check the feasibility of the above mentioned items and if they cannot be implemented, what could be offered as the best alternative.

## Solution Given
Responses:
Stock Visibility: Not feasible currently because stock updates are managed manually by vendors, and real-time sync is unavailable. The platform already shows “Out of Stock” when items are unavailable.
Delivery Tracking: This feature is in progress. Users will soon be able to track shipments via a hyperlink redirecting to the delivery partner’s tracking page.
