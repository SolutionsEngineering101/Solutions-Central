## Problem Description
It has been noted that the platform’s announcement feature currently lacks the option to schedule announcements for a future date. 
For example, if the HR POC has a planned vacation starting in two days and wishes to publish an announcement seven days later (during their time off), there is no way to set this up in advance at the moment. While it appears that scheduling a non-monetary appreciation is currently possible, I hope a similar functionality can be extended to the Announcement feature as well.

Enabling the scheduling option would help minimize the chances of human error, such as HR missing the announcement deadline, and would also be beneficial in situations where the HR POC is unavailable during that time.
