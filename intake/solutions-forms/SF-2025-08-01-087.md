# Solution Request #87 - Tata Chemicals
**Date:** 2025-08-01  |  **Status:** Rejected  |  **Complexity:** Medium Complexity

## Subject
Custom Requirement from Tata Chemicals – Entity-based Setup for Awards and Service Yearbook

## Brief
Customization in award workflow and Service Yearbook setup for multi-entity configuration.

## Problem Description
Tata Chemicals, a long-standing client, follows a non-standard award workflow where nomination and approval roles are flexibly assigned without predefined relationships. Currently, approvers are manually configured under each department via the ‘Manage Department’ section.

With the addition of a new child entity under the same instance, this manual process has become inefficient. The client is requesting a parent-child structure to streamline configuration across multiple entities, reduce manual overhead, and support cross-entity recognition without creating separate instances.

Additionally, from a Service Yearbook (SY) perspective, Tata Chemicals is considering two separate SY programs due to differing leadership and policies between entities. While a unified SY setup is currently proposed, the feasibility of supporting entity-wise SY configurations (e.g., through advanced filtering) is being explored for future scalability.

Key Actions Required:

Assess the feasibility of implementing a parent-child award workflow configuration.

Evaluate technical effort and roadmap impact of supporting entity-wise Service Yearbook configurations.
