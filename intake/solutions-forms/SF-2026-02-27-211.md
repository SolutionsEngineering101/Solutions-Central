## Subject
Quota related

## Brief
Customization in Automatic Quota Allocation

## Problem Description
This requirement concerns Automatic Quota Allocation for users. This is about the client Opella. The client allocates quotas to every employee for specific awards. Fresh quotas are allocated every quarter, and remaining quotas are carried forward every quarter and lapse (if unused) at the end of the calendar year. However, when a new employee joins the org mid-cycle, they must wait until the next allocation cycle (i.e., the next quarter) to receive their quota, unless we manually allocate it. Would like to request a solution that automatically allocates quotas to new users when they join the company and are onboarded onto the platform, based on their grade group.
