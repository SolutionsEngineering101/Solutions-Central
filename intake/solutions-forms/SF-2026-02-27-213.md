# Solution Request #213 - NA Clients
**Date:** 2026-02-27  |  **Status:** Solution Given Closed  |  **Complexity:** Low Complexity

## Subject
Solution request:217213:Birthday notification in email

## Brief
Birthday notification in email

## Problem Description
Most of our clients track employee birthdays; however, the system currently does not have an automated birthday notification trigger. There is no feature that notifies the organization when an employee is celebrating their birthday and prompts others to wish them.

It would be helpful to have a configurable trigger where employees can choose the notification audience-whether it should go to their immediate team, their department, or the entire organization.

## Solution Given
The platform currently cannot support dynamic, employee‑level audience selection for automated birthday announcements; only fixed audience groups can be configured. While birthday emails are sent directly to the employee, additional CC recipients can be added if their details exist in the master data. We have asked Kamalika to confirm whether this fixed audience configuration meets their requirement.
