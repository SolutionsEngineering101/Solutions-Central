# Solution Request #84 - Univo Edtech
**Date:** 2025-07-30  |  **Status:** Solution Given Closed  |  **Complexity:** Low Complexity

## Subject
Solution request 9084::Disabling Competitor EdTech Offers on the Perks Platform for Univo

## Brief
Exclusion of EdTech-specific Offers from competitor brands in Perks Platform for Univo Edtech

## Problem Description
Univo Edtech is evaluating the Vantage Perks platform for their employees. However, since they are an EdTech company themselves, they have raised a concern about the presence of discounts and offers related to other EdTech brands on the platform.

They view this as a potential business conflict and would like the ability to exclude or opt out of EdTech-related offers in their instance of the Perks platform.

Action Required:
Requesting the solutions/product team to assess if it’s possible to:

Filter or exclude specific categories (e.g., "Education" or "Learning") from appearing to Univo Edtech employees

Or, alternatively, disable visibility of specific brand offers considered competitors

Please confirm feasibility and effort estimation, if applicable.

## Solution Given
The necessary checks in coordination with the technical team are done and confirmed that it is feasible to remove exclusive , cashback and all other offers related to other EdTech brands.The client Univo Edtech hasnot conformed on this as the client is not yet on-boarded
