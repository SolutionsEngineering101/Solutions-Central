# Solution Request #232 - Novel Jewels
**Date:** 2026-04-06  |  **Status:** Unknown  |  **Complexity:** Not Set

## Brief
Novel Jewels (Comp id 1894) Requirement is that for one of the awards - GeM Individual and Team, there should be an option to get the previous quarter's date reflecting in the certificate

## Problem Description
As per their process, nominations for Gem - Individual and Gem - Team are raised after the completion of the performance quarter. Accordingly, nominations in April 2026 will pertain to the quarter January–March 2026. However, the certificate currently reflects the nomination date.  
We require a configuration change so that the certificate reflects the relevant performance quarter instead of the nomination date. For example, a nomination raised in January 2026 should display Q3 2025 (October–December 2025) on the certificate.
