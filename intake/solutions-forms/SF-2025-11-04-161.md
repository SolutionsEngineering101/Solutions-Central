# Solution Request #161 - Wipro
**Date:** 2025-11-04  |  **Status:** Solution Given Closed  |  **Complexity:** Low Complexity

## Subject
Solution Request::164161::Customization of First Trigger Email to Users for Service Year Book-Wipro

## Brief
This is regarding the customisation of the first email that gets triggered to user for SY for Wipro.

## Problem Description
For Service Yearbook, the first email is sent to the user 28 days prior to their service anniversary, copying the RM. In that email, the following line is currently mentioned:

“In case you don’t wish to receive your service yearbook, go to the ‘Edit Profile’ section in your profile and uncheck the box that says ‘Show my anniversary on social feed’.”

The client has requested to update this line with the detailed navigation steps as below:

“In case you don’t wish to receive your service yearbook, go to the profile icon at the top right corner > select View Profile > select My Account Preference > uncheck the box that says ‘Activate Service Yearbook and Work Anniversary communication on the feed in the platform'."

## Solution Given
A Jira ticket VC-13981 is created for Gayatri to do the same.
