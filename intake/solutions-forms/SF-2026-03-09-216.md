# Solution Request #216 - Bata
**Date:** 2026-03-09  |  **Status:** Solution Given Closed  |  **Complexity:** Low

## Subject
Solution request::220216::Customised award nomination workflow for annual awards for the client Bata.

## Brief
Customised award nomination workflow for annual awards for the client Bata.

## Problem Description
Requirement Overview:

Bata would like to configure an Annual Award within the platform. As part of the nomination process, they require a custom form to capture specific information related to the nominee’s KPIs and performance criteria. The details collected through the form will be used by leadership for evaluation.

Proposed Flow:

Nominator submits nomination for the Annual Award.

Nominator selects the “Annual Award” category from the award list.

Upon selection, a custom form appears where the nominator can provide required inputs related to predefined criteria (e.g., KPIs, achievements, performance highlights, etc.).

Submitted form details are captured and shared with the CEO for review.

CEO reviews the nominations along with the submitted form details.

CEO selects and approves the top 3 winners based on the information provided in the form.

## Solution Given
After reviewing the request, we would like to highlight that a custom nomination form that appears for the nominator during submission is currently not supported in the platform.
However, the following alternative can be considered to achieve a similar outcome:
Using the Recognition Message & Attachment
The nominator can provide detailed information related to the nominee’s KPIs, achievements, and performance highlights in the Recognition Message section while submitting the nomination.
Additionally, the nominator can upload a supporting document or form containing structured details as an attachment.
This information will then be available for approver to review during the evaluation process.
