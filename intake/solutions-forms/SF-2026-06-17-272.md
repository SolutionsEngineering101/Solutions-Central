# Solution Request #272 - Ultratech Wires and Cables
**Date:** 2026-06-17  |  **Status:** Unknown  |  **Complexity:** Not Set

## Brief
Ultratech Wires & Cables is currently under implementation, and they have requested a Publish feature for Standard and Panel Awards.

What this feature entails:

After the award is approved in the frontend by the final approver or winner selector, they would have a Publish option

By clicking this button only, it will:

Publish the feed

Trigger the email

Award the points

Reason for the request:
Ultratech wants to park the final winners to be announced together on a particular day as an event within the organisation. This gives them control over when to publicly announce the winners rather than having them published immediately upon approval.
