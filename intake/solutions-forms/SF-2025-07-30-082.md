## Subject
Solution Request 9184::Customised Experiences on the platform for HUL

## Brief
Customized Experiences on the platform for HUL

## Problem Description
Is there scope for us to suggest and help curate the experience specifically for HUL?

## Solution Given
Based on our discussion with Piyush, HUL aims to enable the Experience feature with a key requirement that all offers must be valid only within India. We explored two options:
Hammock Holidays
Supports resort stays within India.
Ideal for domestic travel.
Does not cover dining or other experience types.
Switchfly
Offers a wide range of experiences (dining, holidays, activities).
Supports both domestic and international options.
Provides more variety but doesn't restrict to India.
