## Solution Given
Ticker Band on Home Page – While we currently do not support a running ticker, we do offer two types of banner placements:
A rectangular banner on top of the homepage, or

A right-side panel banner beside the feed.
Either of these can be used to convey short messages  prominently.

AV in Announcement on News Feed Page – Audio-visual content (e.g., videos) can be embedded in announcements. However, please note that this content will open and play in a separate browser tab to ensure optimal performance across devices.
GIF Banner – This is supported. We recommend optimized GIFs to maintain platform speed and responsiveness ideally less than 500kb
Team Leaderboards – Based on our conversation, it seems the organization has multiple teams and would like to visualize overall team standings (e.g., Team A – 1st, Team B – 5th).
Unfortunately, we do not support cross-team ranking in that format currently.
However, we can implement a third-level filter (e.g., Department, Zone, or Team Name) within the feed and leaderboard. This would allow users to view their personal ranking within their own team context.
Example:
Suppose Rohan is part of Team B. When he accesses the leaderboard, applying the 'Team B' filter will allow him to see where he stands compared to others in his team—e.g., Rohan is ranked 3rd out of 20.
While this doesn't rank teams against each other, it gives meaningful insight at an individual-team level.
