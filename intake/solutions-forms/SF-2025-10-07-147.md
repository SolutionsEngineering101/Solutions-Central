## Subject
Solution Request 152147::Feasibility of Increasing CSV Upload Limit to 400

## Brief
Increasing CSV Upload Limit to 400

## Problem Description
Checking the feasibility of increasing the CSV upload limit to 400, so that even if 400 users are uploaded at once, the feed is generated, rather than only generating feeds for groups of 50 users.

## Solution Given
The platform already supports CSV uploads of up to 500 users, so uploading 400 users is feasible.
However, for feed generation, there's a limit of 50 users per feed group.
To successfully generate feeds for 400 users, they must be divided into groups of 50 or fewer.
