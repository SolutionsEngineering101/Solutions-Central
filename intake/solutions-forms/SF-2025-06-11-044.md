# Solution Request #44 - ltts
**Date:** 2025-06-11  |  **Status:** Rejected  |  **Complexity:** High Complexity

## Subject
Solution Request Milestone Template and Profile Picture

## Brief
Profile photo update

## Problem Description
update profile photo of all the employees  1) Bulk upload 2) Directly uploaded by employees & points awarded

## Solution Given
We consulted with the Tech team regarding this. Unfortunately, bulk uploading of photos is not feasible due to several technical and compliance-related reasons:
It violates our Data Security and Privacy policies, including GDPR compliance.
Our system uses Cloudinary to store images, generating a unique link for each photo upon user upload. These links are then saved in our database. This process must be initiated by the user and cannot be performed on their behalf.
Image extraction through HRMS/HRIS integration is also not possible, as those systems may store profile pictures in formats that are incompatible with our Cloudinary-based storage requirements.
The same response applies to the recent query regarding bulk upload of profile photos for LTTS.
