# Solution Request #156 - MSAF
**Date:** 2025-10-28  |  **Status:** Solution Given Closed  |  **Complexity:** Low Complexity

## Brief
Need to disable the quota mail scheduler for MSAF, as the recent enhancement triggered email notifications to users about unused quotas. The client has requested to stop these communications for MSAF.

## Problem Description
The recent product enhancement triggered automated emails to all users holding unused quotas, encouraging them to utilize their balance. While this is a standard feature implemented across all clients to increase engagement on the platform, MSAF has requested to discontinue these email notifications.

To address this, we need to disable the quota mail scheduler specifically for MSAF so that no further automated emails are sent to their users regarding unused quotas.

Key Actions Required:

Identify and exclude MSAF from the global quota mail scheduler.

Ensure that no future quota reminder communications are triggered for MSAF.

Validate post-deployment that no emails are sent to MSAF users for unused quota notifications.
