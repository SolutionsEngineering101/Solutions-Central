## Problem Description
The client has requested a feature to notify an entire department via email when one of its members receives an award. For instance, if the award recipient is from After Sales, all employees within the After Sales department should receive a notification. The intent is to encourage colleagues to like and comment on approved nominations and thereby drive higher participation, as a majority of employees have not yet logged in to the portal.

## Solution Given
Our understanding: The client wants recognition emails to be sent to the entire department to increase engagement. The core issue is low login behavior - many users are not logging into the platform, so they are missing in-platform notifications. 

While bulk emails may improve visibility, we do not recommend this approach due to the increase in recurring email costs (separate from any custom development effort).
Given the cost implications, a detailed cost analysis would be required before considering bulk email as a solution.


Based on the data from the past six months, sending the email individually to every employee in the department would result in an average of approximately 5000 extra emails.
We have discussed this with the Ops team, and a more cost-effective approach would be to use a distribution list, as it is counted as a single email.
Under this approach, the client can create a distribution list for each department and share it with us. While this may still increase the overall overhead, the impact would be significantly lower compared to adding each employee in the department to the CC matrix.
