# Solution Request #103 - Coforge
**Date:** 2025-08-12  |  **Status:** Solution Given Closed  |  **Complexity:** Low Complexity

## Subject
Solution Request 110103:: Feasibility Check Requests from Client Coforge on Platform Capabilities

## Brief
We have received some queries from our client to check for feasibility on the platform.

## Problem Description
Please review the below queries from Coforge and advise on feasibility, available options, and any dependencies:

1. Integration with Internal Platform (IEngage)

- Possibility of integrating the Vantage Recognition solution with Coforge’s internal IEngage platform.

- Required details: Integration method, data exchange scope, and any limitations.

2. Manager Gifting – Wallet Top-Up

- In the Manager gifting feature, can managers personally top up the wallet amount from their side, instead of the company funding the wallet?

- Required details: Process flow, permissions, and any compliance considerations.

3. Integration with Yammer

- Capability to integrate Vantage Recognition with Microsoft Yammer for recognition posts/notifications.

- Required details: Possible integration approach, scope, and dependencies.

4. Budget Segregation by Location

- Can BU/department budgets be further segregated from country level to city or region level?

- Required details: System capability, reporting impact, and configuration effort.

## Solution Given
Response from Solutions team are in blue font.   Integration with Internal Platform (IEngage)
Could you please help us understand the scope of this integration and provide more details about the IEngage platform?

2. Manager Gifting – Wallet Top-Up
- In the Manager gifting feature, can managers personally top up the wallet amount from their side, instead of the company funding the wallet?
No, this is not supported. Wallet top-ups are strictly budget-driven and can only be funded through company-assigned budgets.

3. Integration with Yammer
- Capability to integrate Vantage Recognition with Microsoft Yammer for recognition posts/notifications.
Yes, this integration is feasible and will be managed by our tech team.

4. Budget Segregation by Location
- Can BU/department budgets be further segregated from country level to city or region level?
Yes, this is possible. If the client can provide the relevant stakeholder details—such as country heads, city heads, or region heads—we can allocate budgets accordingly to each level. This allows for more granular control over budget distribution based on organizational structure.
