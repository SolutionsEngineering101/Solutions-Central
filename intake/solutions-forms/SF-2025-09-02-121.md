**Date:** 2025-09-02  |  **Status:** Solution Given Closed  |  **Complexity:** Low Complexity

## Subject
Solution Request 128121 American Oncology Association - Employee Search by ID

## Brief
One of my prospects wants to know whether users can find their peers using employee IDs (apart from name and email) at the time of appreciation or nomination. This ensures accuracy, especially in larger organizations where there may be duplicate or similar names, making the appreciation or nomination process smoother and error-free.

## Problem Description
One of my prospects, American Oncology Institute from India, wants to know whether users can find their peers using employee IDs (apart from name and email) at the time of appreciation or nomination. This ensures accuracy, especially in larger organizations where there may be duplicate or similar names, making the appreciation or nomination process smoother and error-free.

## Solution Given
We consulted with the Tech team and confirmed that the platform does not currently support employee search by ID.
While the concern is valid, the issue is currently mitigated by using email IDs, which serve as unique identifiers - especially in cases where multiple individuals share the same name.
