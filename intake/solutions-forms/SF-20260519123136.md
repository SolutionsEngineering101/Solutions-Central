## Brief Requirement for project-wise quota allocation or multiple quota assignment for a single HOD in the Shining Star award workflow.


## DescriptionThe client requires flexibility in quota allocation for HODs managing multiple projects. Currently, for Shining Star award the quota is configured to be allocated as 1 quota per month to the HODs, which limits a single HOD to a fixed quota assignment.

In this case, one HOD oversees three different projects and needs to approve three nominations, one for each project. So the client is requesting to check if we can enable project-wise quota allocation.

## Priority JustificationPriority: Medium

Justification:
The client has a business requirement where a single HOD manages multiple projects and needs the ability to approve separate quotas for each project under the Shining Star award. Enabling multiple quota assignments or project-wise quota mapping would help streamline the approval process, reduce administrative overhead, and better align the platform with the client’s organizational structure.
