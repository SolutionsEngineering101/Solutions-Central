# Solution Request #228 - QX GLOBAL
**Date:** 2026-03-27  |  **Status:** Solution Given Closed  |  **Complexity:** Low Complexity

## Subject
Solution Request 232228 QX Global

## Brief
Customisation in the award workflow

## Problem Description
1. Moderator functionality to approve, reject, or block content containing profanity, whether in English or any regional language. In hashtags and asterisk marks also.
2. The Monthly and Spot Awards will be held until the end of the month and disbursed in bulk on the last Friday of each month, or on a customizable date.
For example, if the last Friday falls on a holiday, there should be flexibility to modify and select an alternative date accordingly.
3. Similar to point number 2, once the panel voting is completed, all online postings should be scheduled for the end of the month. Points allocation and notifications should also be triggered on the same day.
Additionally, all awards should be disbursed in a single batch rather than individually, avoiding multiple clicks for multiple awards. This is important as the intent is to celebrate the awards offline first and then announce them online. Can this be automated?
4. Profanity filtering mechanism for regional languages also.

## Solution Given
AI Detection of foul language/profanity in recognition messages, including regional languages. Answer: The AI recognition guide, currently being enhanced by the Product Team, will be able to detect foul language and profanity. Expected to be completed by Q1 FY 26-27.

Moderator facility to reject or block such content. Answer: Moderation is not currently available and is not on the immediate roadmap. However, as a workaround, an additional HR approval layer can be introduced for awards only to enable manual review before publishing.

One click bulk award publication for monthly R&R programs at the end of the Month or designated date. Answer: By default, auto-approved awards are published instantly. If the organization prefers scheduled publishing (e.g., end of the month), this can be managed by introducing an HR approval step. The approver can then use the bulk approval feature to publish all awards at once.

Panel award announcement and associated point allocation, notification to go out on the day decided by the client. Answer: Panel award announcements are triggered only after the Winner Selector finalizes the winners. The selection timeline can be configured to align with the organization’s preferred announcement date.

All Panel awards to be disbursed in a single batch, instead of separate awards. Answer: Panel awards require manual confirmation by the Winner Selector. As such, awards cannot be automatically disbursed in a single batch and must be processed individually.

Two-way HRIS capability. Answer: Currently, the platform primarily consumes employee data shared by the client for program execution. Two-way integration can be explored based on technical feasibility and will be confirmed by our Tech team.

Budget allocation automation by Manager, Grade, Reportees and BU. Answer: The platform supports automated budget allocation based on multiple parameters, including country, allocation frequency, designation, grade, department, and number of reportees.

Native language filtering capability. Answer: Need more clarity on the ask.
