## Brief Blue-collar workforce engagement and rewards solution for contractual employees across manufacturing units with mobile-first access, offline-friendly recognition, and milestone-based gifting support.


## DescriptionJindal Steel is exploring an R&R solution for ~20,000 contractual blue-collar employees at their Angul, Odisha plant, with future scalability up to 30–35k employees. The objective is to build an inclusive engagement ecosystem for a workforce with limited email/internet access and they need a blue-print of Vantage Circle approach to the blue-collared workforce enagegement.

Key expected outcomes from the platform:

Mobile number + OTP-based employee access
Local language communication and notifications
Celebration of employee life milestones (birthdays, marriage, childbirth, etc.)
Floor-level spot recognition mechanism through physical/digital “Shabash” cards
Centralized points/rewards wallet for employee redemption
End-to-end gifting support and fulfillment for milestone occasions
Backend eligibility management through CSV/ERP/HRMS integration
Redemption ecosystem covering gift cards, merchandise, and company-curated rewards

The client is looking for a simple, scalable, and operationally low-touch solution tailored for manufacturing and blue-collar workforce engagement.
