## Brief
2. The ability to edit the attachment included in a submitted nomination. This request stems from the fact that the client requires certain mandatory attachments to be included with every nomination. However, they’ve noticed that nominators often upload incorrect files. When such nominations are declined and nominators are asked to resubmit, they usually don't take any further action—leading to missed nominations.

## Problem Description
My client, Addverb Technologies, has mentioned that their assigned nominators are not submitting nominations as frequently as expected. To help improve the nomination rate, the client is requesting two features:

An automated email reminder (nudge) sent from the system to prompt nominators to submit nominations. Please note the client follows a No Budget Concept.

The ability to edit the attachment included in a submitted nomination. This request stems from the fact that the client requires certain mandatory attachments to be included with every nomination. However, they’ve noticed that nominators often upload incorrect files. When such nominations are declined and nominators are asked to resubmit, they usually don't take any further action—leading to missed nominations.

## Solution Given
As for the second query, we already have a 'Reinitiate' feature available (screenshot attached). This feature enables the nominator to edit the whole nomination, be it the award, amount, citations, or attachments.
