# Solution Request #138 - National Food Product Corporation
**Date:** 2025-09-25  |  **Status:** Solution Given Closed  |  **Complexity:** Medium Complexity

## Subject
Solution Request 143138::Feasibility Check for NFPC UAE R&R Workflow Requirements

## Brief
1.NFPC would like to allow approvers the ability to modify or decide the final award value proposed by nominators during the approval process. They have six levels of approvals and they have requested the 3rd level approver to have the eligibility to modify the awards values selected by the nominator.
2. Multiple award amounts required to configure.
3. Six level of approval for workflow

## Problem Description
1. NFPC wants that the award value can be modified by the approver after the nominators have provided during the approval process.
2. Multiple award amounts to be put in drop down like 25 of the award denominations to be configured.
3. While we recommended limiting the workflow to two or three approval levels for efficiency, the NFPC team has requested a six-level approval workflow.
Their justification:
"The same approval hierarchy is defined considering our diversified operations across various locations, and validation is required from the respective location finance teams on employee achievements."
