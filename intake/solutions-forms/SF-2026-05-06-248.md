# Solution Request #248 - Servier
**Date:** 2026-05-06  |  **Status:** Unknown  |  **Complexity:** Not Set

## Brief
Nominator identifier to be set to 2 parameters: Reporting Managers and Grade Group

## Problem Description
The client Servier for one of their award workflow has requested that the eligible nominators would be Reporting Managers (Individuals with team/s) and Functional Head (who are department heads). For functional heads, they have a unique identifiable grade group; however, for the reporting managers, not all of them fall under unique grade group. Additionally, they have a grade group that has a mixture of Reporting Managers and non-Reporting Managers. To address this, we have proposed a couple of approaches : 

- To pick up the functional head details in the additional info columns, but the client has highlighted HRMS integration issues
- To individually fill in the details of the eligible nominators in the award configuration via the dashboard. However, the client has highlighted that they want the award workflow in a way where functional heads can nominate other functional heads and reporting managers and reporting managers can nominate other reporting managers but not functional heads

We request your support in solutioning this requirement that can help us achieve the request while minimising manual maintenance.
