## Subject
Solution request 7468::Feasibility check for Customisation of 'Year' calculation of Leaderboard

## Brief
¸

## Problem Description
The client has requested to change the 'Year' calculation of LEaderboard to be Finalcial year instead of the Calender year.
The reason of this request is that the RnR program that runs within the organisation is entirely based on Financial year.

## Solution Given
After conducting a thorough review with the technical team, we conclude that transitioning from the calendar year to the fiscal year is a complex change that will require significant time and effort. If the fiscal year is implemented, there could be issues with the user experience, as leaderboard data may appear empty or inconsistent at the beginning of each new fiscal year.
