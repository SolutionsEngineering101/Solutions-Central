## Subject
Solution request245241 ::Function-wise R&R with restricted visibility and participation controls

## Brief
Function-wise R&R with restricted visibility and participation controls

## Problem Description
Description

The client wants the ability to run two types of R&R programs in parallel within the platform, with clear control over visibility and participation.

Let’s assume an organization has two functions:

* Sales
* Manufacturing
---

Scenario 1: Organization-wide R&R

The company launches an award like “Employee of the Quarter.”

 The award is visible to all employees across the organization
  All employees can:

   Participate and nominate others
   View the award in the platform (when it gets posted in the feed)

---

Scenario 2: Function-wise R&R (Sales Only)

The company launches an award like “Sales Champion.”

 The award is visible only to the Sales team
 Only Sales team members can:

   Participate and nominate others
   View the award in the platform (when it gets posted in the feed)

---

What this means

 An employee from Manufacturing:

  Cannot see the “Sales Champion” award in their feed or anywhere on the platform
  Cannot participate or nominate in this program

Similarly, Sales employees will not have visibility or access to function-specific R&R programs created for other teams.
